About Amax

Amax was created for Hack the 6ix 2018 by Nishkirt Desai, Connor McCallum, Steven Xu, and Andy Yang. Machine learning techniques were used to create the questions and answers, summary and important words. Amax can be used to strengthen one's understanding of information by quizzing themself, reviewing the summary and inquiring about the important words. Examples of information that could be inserted are paragraphs from textbook, such as biology or history, information froms encyclopedias, news articles, biographies and much more.

Thank you for using Amax.
